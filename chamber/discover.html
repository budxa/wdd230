Discover Greendale

Greendale is a lovely town found in the province of Harare, Zimbabwe. It is known for its tranquillity, beauty and just as its name implies, for its overwhelming dominant colour of green in terms of nature. This page provides an overview of the town’s information along with pictures of the town.

Events

The residents of Greendale have made it a norm to host conventions every year where people get to showcase their art designs, technical prototypes, and books such as novels, comics, etc. In addition, the community hosts sporting activities of all sorts as a way to strengthen the bond of the people as they get to interact with one another every three months , though some individuals do it every weekend.

Economic Profile

The economic aspects of Greendale have been improving throughout the years as it has managed to attract businesses and investors that have brought in a lot of traffic in the town as there had never been much of a big market to begin with.

History

The town’s history is not well known by many, but it was never really intended to be expanded into something big as it is now. Also, it used to be a place that specifically reserved for individuals of the upper class alone with only real-estate investment in mind.

Education

From the time of its establishment till now there are now 4 major elementary schools, 5 middle schools and 3 high schools that have been built and all of them hold fantastic records throughout their districts with exceptional, maintained facilities.

Geographic Highlights

The geographic setting of the town at a glance is seen to have been a well thought idea as it is situated close to major towns and benefit having to be not to far away from the local zoo. As its name implies, its entire natural surroundings are ever green from the trees to the grassy fields all year out. In a addition, there is a river that is actually close by.
